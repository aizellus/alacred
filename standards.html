Category Details

Each category should be visible enough to explain and practical enough to audit.

Trust and Transparency

Business identity consistency, clear contact information, understandable service terms, pricing approach, and a visible complaint path.

Customer Experience

Prompt response, appointment clarity, communication updates, follow-up habits, and complaint resolution windows.

Operational Reliability

Defined roles, scheduling processes, quality checks, documentation habits, and rework handling.

Safety and Preparedness

Safety expectations, incident response, relevant coverage proof, and procedural awareness across staff.

Workforce and Culture

Onboarding, training cadence, conduct standards, accountability norms, and documented workplace expectations.

Community and Digital Trust

Local participation, partnerships, giving, current web presence, matching listings, review responsiveness, and usable contact flows.

Evidence Model

The proof should be concrete, reviewable, and lightweight enough to scale.
